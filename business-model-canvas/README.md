# business-model-canvas

## What it does
Builds a complete Business Model Canvas with all 9 blocks for any named business or idea — new venture, existing company audit, or investor materials. Best used when you need a structured view of how a business creates, delivers, and captures value, and want it delivered as a PDF with a Key Assumptions & Risks section.

## Reference files
None.

## Install
Copy this folder into your skills directory, then toggle the skill on in **Customize > Skills**.

## What to change
None. The skill asks for business context at runtime via Gate 1 before drafting anything.

## Usage
**Trigger:** "Create a business model canvas for [company or idea]"
**Output:** PDF (or markdown fallback) with all 9 BMC blocks and a Key Assumptions & Risks section, saved to [your output folder].
